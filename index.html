We are the first and only international honor society for the Computing Sciences (CS). The UC Berkeley chapter launched in 1995 and upholds the tradition of academic excellence by honoring the top tier of CS students.

We serve the CS and Engineering communities with student-led workshops, advising and tutoring programs, lecture series featuring inspiring visionaries in the field, and more.

Companies can purchase a UPE Resumé Book from our organization, a portfolio of resumés with nearly 30 resumés of Berkeley's highest-achieving computer science students. You can use our resumés as a starting point for your company's recruiting process.
